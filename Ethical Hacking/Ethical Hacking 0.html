Who are the hackers?

As the term #hackerr" strikes our mind, the 1st thing that comes in our mind is cybercrime. We witness news flashing every day on #cybercrimes – through the mail, social networking sites, phone numbers, and more.

  • White_Hat_hackers(legal): A white-hat hacker is a person who #breaks into systems ethically. The purpose is not to steal info. but to enhance or test the security. They are legally permitted to do the same and are often called as ethical hackers. In fact, there are professional certifications that certify one as a Certified Ethical Hacker.

  • Black_Hat_hackers(illegal): Not all hackers are crackers. Now, opposite of white hat hackers, they break into systems due to #malicious intent. They use private information of people for causing crimes like #robbery, #fraud, #vandalism, and more which is punishable by law.

  • Grey_Hat_hackers(both): They belong to the grey area and exhibits traits of both white and black hat hackers. These types of hackers usually dig into other systems #unethically while causing no such malicious harm.
