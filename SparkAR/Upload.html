Let's share Online

By Praddyum Verma

27 June 2020

Congrats for creating your first filter and it's time to share you work online with your dear ones. Before anything, do Ctrl+S to save your project. (I believe you can do this :) )
So I assume you tried out your filter via SparkAR Player I mentioned in a previous blog and you are ready to Upload it on your social handles. Follow the steps shown below

If you using it for first time it may ask you to login with your facebook account
Now let's understand what we just did.

    When you press the upload button a pop up will appear with the following options.

  • If your file size is green in all channels it means it's not oversized. In case it's oversized it will be rendered in red color.(Not good for you as you can't upload oversized file)

  • There are two options i.e publish new effect OR update existing effect. Don't tell me you can't understand these simple options.

  • If you click on the Export file it will create your .arexport file and save it on your local machine.

  • For this tutorial we'll be choosing Upload to upload our filter directly to SparkAR Hub
Now here we got two/three (depending upon type of facebook account) i.e Upload to facebook or Instagram.

Tip: Do read these terms and conditions for hazel free upload Click Here!

  • Gave it a name

  • Now we need to upload a preview video of our filter which is recorded directly from our instagram. To do that follow the steps.

  • Now you need to provide an icon. But do read the links I provided above if you don't want your effect to be rejected.

  • Give some instruction to reviewer describing your effect and Submit

It will be sent for review and you can see the estimated time on this page. If your filter follow all the SparkAR policies it will be accepted and if not it will be rejected with the reasons and you can again submit it with the necessary changes.
