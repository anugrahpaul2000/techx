Welcome Aboard ! Before we dive deep in this beautiful World of Augmented Reality. Let me introduce you with some important terms :

  • Augmented Reality (AR) is an interactive experience where we alter one's ongoing perception of the real world environment by adding computer generated graphics into its surrounding. I'm damn sure you must have played or at least heard about Pokemon Go!. Actually that's the point from where Augmented Reality revolutionized the Industrial World.

    • Image by David Grandmougin
  • Virtual Reality (VR) is also an interactive experience but in this we completely change the surrounding of the user by blocking it's actual vision via dedicated headsets and showing him only the computer generated graphics. Think of your dreams where you are in a Wonderland but in reality you are sleeping in your bed.

    • Image by JESHOOTS
  • Extended Reality (XR) is an umbrella under which all real-and -virtual combined environments (i.e. AR & VR) & human machine interactions generated by computer technologies and wearables come in. Think of Ready Player One Suit.
    • Image by Yanik

Let's start with SparkAR

So SparkAR is a software by facebook to make filters for Facebook developers, Instagram and Facebook Ads. It's extremely easy to start with but with time requires lots and lots of imagination and Javascript( ES6 ) to create filters of Industry Level.

Don't panic after reading that Javascript is needed. I said it's needed but not necessary.

So without wasting much time lets go!.We need to download two things. Read all the System requirements:

  • Download and install Spark AR on your laptop/desktop Click here!

  • Download and install Spark AR Player on your Mobile phone for testing your cool filters.Click here!

    • Launch the Installer. At the time of installation you need to login with your facebook credentials.
